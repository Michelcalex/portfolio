About Me

I build simple and functional interfaces. I am a problem solver and love to bring ideas to life.

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Advertising and Graphic Arts and Imaging Technology at Appalachian State University in Boone, NC. I have spent the past five years as an IT Recruiter working with Developers and other IT professionals. This helped me gain a lot of knowledge and insight about developer tools, technologies and work environments. With my creative background and love for technology, I knew it was time to combine my passions together, and thus become a Front-End Developer! I have been learning to code since then through various courses and resources. I have completed an 8 week course with Tech Talent South on Web Design and Creation. Currently, I am taking courses with Free Code Camp which will further my skill set in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React, and more!

When I'm not creating things, you can find me outside....probably hammocking, hula-hooping or enjoying a nice cold beer!
